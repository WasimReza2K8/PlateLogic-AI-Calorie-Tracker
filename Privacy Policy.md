# PlateLogic: AI Calorie Tracker Privacy Policy

1. Introduction
PlateLogic: AI Calorie Tracker is a mobile app that helps users track their daily calorie intake using artificial intelligence. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users and this Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

2. Effective Date
This Privacy Policy is effective as of May 5, 2026, and will be updated as necessary to reflect changes in our data collection and usage practices.

3. Data Collection
When you use our app, we collect the following types of data:
    a. Photos of your food or plate: You may choose to upload a photo of your food or plate to our app, which is then provided to our AI model for analysis. Please note that these photos are not stored in our storage and are only used by the AI model to provide you with calorie tracking information. The AI model does not use these photos to train its algorithms.
    b. Usage data: We collect data on how you use our app, including the features you access and the actions you take.

4. Use of Data
We use the data we collect to:
    a. Provide you with calorie tracking information and other features of our app
    b. Improve the accuracy and functionality of our AI model
    c. Analyze usage trends and optimize our app's performance
    d. Comply with applicable laws and regulations

5. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services to collect and analyze data:
    1. Firebase Analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect usage data and analyze app performance.
    2. Firebase Firestore: We use Firebase Firestore to store certain app data.
    3. Gemini AI API: We use the Gemini AI API to provide you with calorie tracking information.
    4. Open AI API: We use the Open AI API to improve the functionality of our AI model.

6. Data Storage and Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction. However, no data transmission or storage can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. We store your data in secure servers and use encryption to protect data in transit.

7. Data Subject Rights
Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have certain rights with respect to your personal data, including:
    a. The right to access your data
    b. The right to rectify or correct your data
    c. The right to erase your data
    d. The right to restrict processing of your data
    e. The right to object to processing of your data
    f. The right to data portability

8. GDPR Compliance
We comply with the GDPR and provide the following information:
    a. Lawful basis for processing: We process your data based on your consent to use our app and to provide you with calorie tracking information.
    b. Data protection officer: We do not have a dedicated data protection officer, but you can contact us at wasim.codeartist.business@gmail.com with any data protection questions or concerns.
    c. International data transfers: We may transfer your data to third-party services located outside the European Economic Area (EEA), but we ensure that such transfers are made in accordance with the GDPR.

9. CCPA Compliance
We comply with the CCPA and provide the following information:
    a. Categories of personal data collected: We collect the categories of data listed in section 3 above.
    b. Purposes for collecting data: We collect data for the purposes listed in section 4 above.
    c. Sale of personal data: We do not sell your personal data.
    d. Rights under the CCPA: You have the right to request access to your data, to request deletion of your data, and to opt-out of the sale of your data (if applicable).

10. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our data collection and usage practices. We will notify you of any changes by posting an updated version of this policy on our website.

11. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at wasim.codeartist.business@gmail.com.
